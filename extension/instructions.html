Welcome to Mochi Chat

Your AI-powered chat companion for PDFs and websites.


Let's get you set up in just a few steps!

Activate Mochi Chat

To start chatting with Mochi, simply press Command+K (Mac) or Ctrl+K (Windows) on any webpage or PDF.

What Mochi Can Do For You

Mochi Chat enhances your browsing and reading experience with powerful AI capabilities:

📝
"Summarize Key Points"
Get concise summaries of articles
📧
"Draft an Email for..."
Create professional emails based on content
🔍
"Find X from the PDF""
Find answers to your questions instantly
💡
"Explain this Concept""
Explain any topics/concepts on the page

Enable File Access & You're All Set!

To chat with PDFs, Mochi needs permission to access local files. Click the button below to open extension settings and enable

"Allow access to file URLs"
